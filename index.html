About Us

Welcome to Blood Club — where the night bleeds music and the shadows hum with sound.

Curated by DX, a figure shrouded in candlelight and distortion, Blood Club is more than a website — it's a sanctuary for the haunted, the heartbroken, and those who find beauty in the dark.

Dripping with the sounds of Casket Cassette, Wicca Phase Springs Eternal, Lil Peep, Traitrs, Ter Nura, Mareux, Soft Kill and others who summon sorrow like a spell, Blood Club pulses with the lifeblood of underground melancholia.

We won’t tell you everything. We don’t need to.

Just know this: if you’ve ever danced alone beneath a dying moon, or felt your heartbeat sync with a drum echoing through fog — you’re already one of us.

Step inside. Stay awhile. The night is eternal.
